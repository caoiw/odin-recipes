Basic Burger

Description

Classic basic burger with a juicy seasoned beef patty, melted cheese, fresh lettuce, tomato, onion, and your favorite condiments, all stacked on a soft toasted bun. Simple, satisfying, and ready in minutes!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices (cheddar or american)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices
  • Condiments: ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Season ground beef patties with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Cook patties for about 3-4 minutes per side for medium doneness (adjust time as needed).
  4. In the last minute of cooking, add cheese slices on top and cover to melt (optional).
  5. Toast burger buns lightly on the grill or skillet.
  6. Assemble burgers by placing the patty on the bun, then add lettuce, tomato, onion, and condiments.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
